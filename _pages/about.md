Xuchuan is a third-year Ph.D. candidate from [BZ Lab](https://appsrv.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~yfzhou/), School of Computer Science, [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/) (co-supervised by [Prof. Yangfan Zhou](https://cs.fudan.edu.cn/3f/a9/c25909a278441/page.htm) and [Prof. Xin Wang](https://cs.fudan.edu.cn/3f/7e/c25906a278398/page.htm)). He received a B.Sc degree in Computer Science from Fudan University in 2021. His research interests are memory disaggregation, RDMA systems and storage systems.
